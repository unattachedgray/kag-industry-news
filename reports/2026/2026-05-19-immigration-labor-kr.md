## 🆕 USCIS 39개국 보류 — 1,200만 건 적체 + 절반 영향

([Boundless](https://www.boundless.com/blog/uscis-expands-immigration-adjudication-pause-39-countries), [Ogletree](https://ogletree.com/insights-resources/blog-posts/uscis-pauses-benefit-requests-for-nationals-from-high-risk-countries-and-halts-asylum-adjudications/))
