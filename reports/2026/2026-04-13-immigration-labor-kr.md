```

-------|------------------------|-------------------|------
I-140  | Immigrant Petition     | 5–22개월           | →
I-485  | Adjustment of Status   | 11–31.5개월        | →
I-765  | EAD (취업 허가증)        | 2–7개월            | ↓ 개선
PERM   | Labor Certification    | ~512일 (17개월)     | → (11월 접수 심사 중)
PWD    | Prevailing Wage Det.   | ~3개월 (1월 접수 심사) | ↓ 개선
I-129  | H-1B/H-2B Petition     | 변동               | →
```

([DOL FLAG](https://flag.dol.gov/processingtimes), [Envoy Global](https://www.envoyglobal.com/insight/perm-processing-updates-issued-by-dol/), [Manifest Law](https://manifestlaw.com/blog/perm-dol-processing-times/))
